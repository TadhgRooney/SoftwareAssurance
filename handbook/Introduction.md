# [our company startup name]'s Software Quality Handbook

## Introduction

- This handbook will outline the software quality practices that developers in our company should follow. It is important that all engineers follow consistent development practices to ensure reliable progress and reduce the likelyhood of defects in production.

This handbook focuses on three key areas that have equal impact on software quality:

- Test Estimation in Scrum
- Code Reviews
- Testing Practices

- Each key area recommend best practices, common mistakes to avoid, and useful links to further improve your knowledge.

- The goal of this handbook is to provide clear and practical guidlines that developers can refer to while working on projects and improve team collaboration, reduce defact, and deliver more reliable software.
