# Task Estimation in Scrum

## Overview
Task estimation in Scrum is the process of forecasting how much effort is required 
to complete items in the product backlog. Teams typically use relative measures like 
story points to assess complexity, risk and effort rather than exact hours.

These resources were used for the Task Estimation section of the handbook.
They were chosen to reflect real world experiences and challenges rather than pure theory.
